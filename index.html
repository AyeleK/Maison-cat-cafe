Our Mission

At Maison Cat Café, our mission is to create a warm, welcoming space where people can relax, enjoy handcrafted coffee and pastries, and spend meaningful time with adoptable cats. In partnership with local shelters, we provide a safe and loving environment for rescued cats while helping them find their forever homes. Through connection, comfort, and community, Maison brings people and pets together, one visit at a time.

Whether you’re stopping by for a quiet moment, browsing our menu, or looking to adopt a new best friend, Maison is a place where love, comfort, and compassion come together.
